Identify and Mitigate Risks Associated with Open Source Intelligence and Human Error

Many organizations are unaware of the extent to which their sensitive information is available online. Attackers can use OSINT techniques to gather information about an organization’s employees, networks, and systems, and use this information to launch targeted attacks. Social engineering tactics, such as phishing and pretexting, exploit human error to trick employees into divulging sensitive information. These types of attacks can have serious consequences, including data breaches, financial loss, and reputational damage.

Open Source Intelligence (OSINT)

OSINT is the practice of gathering and analyzing information from publicly available sources to enhance your cybersecurity posture.

Our OSINT services are designed to meticulously uncover and harness publicly available information that could impact your organization’s security posture. By leveraging state-of-the-art technology and methodologies, we provide: In-depth Digital Footprint Analysis: Discover the extent of your organization’s digital exposure to identify potential vulnerabilities. Competitive Intelligence Gathering: Gain strategic insights into competitors’ activities to stay ahead in your industry. Risk Management and Mitigation: Identify threats and vulnerabilities through comprehensive data analysis, enabling proactive defense strategies.

Our OSINT services include:

  • Public Asset Discovery: Identify and secure your organization’s public-facing assets, such as domains, IP addresses, and social media profiles.
  • Data Aggregation and Analysis: Collect and analyze vast amounts of data to prioritize and address security concerns effectively.
  • Sensitive Information Protection: Locate and secure exposed data that could be exploited by cyber adversaries.

Master the Human Element with Sophisticated Social Engineering

Social Engineering exploits the most unpredictable factor in cybersecurity: human behavior. Our services are crafted to test and enhance your team’s readiness against these deceptive tactics:

  • Customized Phishing Simulations: From email to SMS, our tailored campaigns test your team’s vigilance against phishing attempts.
  • Interactive Training and Awareness Programs: Equip your staff with the knowledge and tools to recognize and respond to social engineering threats.
  • Real-world Attack Simulations: Experience the intensity of a controlled social engineering attack to evaluate your organization’s response protocols.

Compliance and Risk Assessment

Our OSINT and social engineering services also help you measure your organization’s risk and ensure compliance with relevant regulations. We offer:

  • Compliance Assessment: We assess your organization’s compliance with ISO 27001 and other relevant standards, using OSINT techniques to identify potential gaps and vulnerabilities.
  • Risk Assessment: We conduct risk assessments using OSINT sources to identify vulnerabilities in your corporate network and protect your organization from potential threats.

Solution
